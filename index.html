Meet Elizabeth

I grew up in the USA playing video games (Hello, World of Warcraft!), and later relocated to Vancouver and earned an Environmental Science degree. Life led me on a wild journey into landscaping and garden design where I ran my own company for a number of years, until I discovered web development.

I’ve always enjoyed working with computers; Microsoft Excel was my favorite course in university! When Skillcrush popped up on a Google search, I took a chance with their free bootcamp course, and signed up for the front end web development bootcamp that night. I love learning how to code, and am excited to continue learning throughout my career (looking at you, Backend Languages!).

When I’m not at my desk listening to lo-fi beats and coding up a storm, you'll find me in a hammock reading a book, or exploring the mountains with my dogs.
