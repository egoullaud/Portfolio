How I work...

Discover

I start by exploring the scope of the project (AKA I ask QUESTIONS!). Who is the audience? Is this a greenfield project or are we working with an existing product? What are the project goals? Which tools and resources can I utilize? These questions provide me with a framework to begin finding answers and resources.

Research

The next step is all about research. Finding answers to discovery inquiries, gathering resources, ideas, and inspiration for design and content. Think lists and lots of them!

Build

This is where the magic happens - building your website by hand using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React! Once the build is complete, I test each feature across multiple devices and browsers.
